About Ocho

Ocho is an exciting online card game where you must use smart tactics to get rid of all your cards before your opponents. Inspired by the classic UNO game, Ocho brings a fresh experience with a modern interface, dynamic rules and many diverse modes.

How to play Ocho

The goal is to get rid of all of your cards first.

Each person gets seven cards to begin with. If the number or colour of the card on top of the centre pile matches yours, you can play one card. Take one more card from the draw pile if you can't play. You have to press "Ocho" when you only have one card left in your hand.

Special cards in Ocho

Reverse: Reverse the direction of play.

Skip: Skip the next player's turn.

Draw 2: Force the next player to draw 2 cards.

Wild: Change the colour of the card being played.

Wild Draw 4: Change the colour, and the next player draws 4 cards.

Ocho: Special card, can be used flexibly depending on the mode.

Have fun playing Ocho!
